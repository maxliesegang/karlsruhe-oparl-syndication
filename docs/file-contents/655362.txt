Scope 1 - direct emissionsScope 2 - indirect emissionsScope 3 - indirect value chain emissions
Includes all direct greenhouse gas 
emissions, such as primary energy  
sources consumed directly in corporate  
real estate.  
Examples include e.g. datacenter power,  
heating oil,diesel and combustion company  
vehicles.
Includes the indirect greenhouse gas  
emissions resulting from the production  
of the acquired energy.  
The CO2 emissions raise from consumed  
secondary energy sources, such as  
electricity, district heating or cooling  
energy in buildings as well as in electric  
vehicles.
Includes other indirect greenhouse gas  
emissions that are primarily associated  
with the company's activities.  
Scope 3 emissions are divided into 15  
categories, each of which is classified as  
upstream and downstream. 
 
E.g. Power consumption on end devices, 
Usage of CDN (for rented servers outside  
the own data center)
